Sultan ibn Salman Sultan ibn Salman Al Saud, in full Prince Sultan ibn Salman Abd al-Aziz Al Saud,also called Sultan Salman Abdulaziz Al Saud, (born June 27, 1956, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia), astronaut who was the first Saudi Arabian citizen, the first Arab, the first Muslim, and the first member of a royal family to travel into space.
